## Philosophy

**Email is a utility, like the power grid.** Plug in the lamp, it turns on. Doesn't matter how. It just has to work. Easy for two people; hard for millions — that's where the interesting problems live.

**Scale changes the character of problems**, not just their size. Things that break at a million users aren't just bigger versions of what breaks at a thousand. This lens applies directly to Heru: currently hundreds of devices, eventually thousands or tens of thousands. Scaling and availability are the most important *and* most arcane problems to solve.
